C8 Elite Trading Dashboard Integrated with Jenkins Signal Notifications

This is a demonstration of how the C8 Elite trading dashboard can be integrated with Jenkins for receiving signal notifications.

Features

  • Real-time trading signals
  • Automated trading execution
  • Customizable notification settings

How to Integrate

Follow these steps to integrate your C8 Elite trading dashboard with Jenkins:

  1. Set up Jenkins and create a new job.
  2. Configure your Jenkins job to trigger on trading signals.
  3. Use webhooks to send notifications to your C8 Elite dashboard.
  4. Verify the integration by testing the signal notifications.

For more details, visit the official documentation of C8 Elite and Jenkins.
